# Quick start

This guide walks through the core Vala workflow from creating a veteran to preparing claim documents.

<Note>
Use a representative test record or an active matter your organization has approved for onboarding.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a veteran">
    Open **Veterans**, select **New veteran**, and enter the available profile information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the record">
    Add the veteran's C-File and supporting records. Larger files may continue processing after the upload completes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the file">
    Use Chat with Vala to ask questions across the record and verify answers against source citations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize conditions and evidence">
    Review known and potential conditions, supporting evidence, and any gaps that need follow-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate reports and forms">
    Create the appropriate report, populate VA forms, and review every generated output before use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect signatures">
    Send documents through vSign when a veteran or other signer needs to complete or approve them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Vala assists accredited representatives. Review all AI-generated analysis, extracted information, and completed forms before relying on or submitting them.
</Warning>

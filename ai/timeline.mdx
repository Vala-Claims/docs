## Overview

Timeline supports chronological review of information available for a veteran matter.

## When to use

Use Timeline when sequence, timing, or changes over time matter to your review.

## Prerequisites

- The correct veteran workspace
- Relevant records available for review

## Best practices

- Review surrounding events, not only isolated entries.
- Distinguish record dates from dates mentioned within a record.
- Verify consequential dates independently.

## Common mistakes

- Assuming every event is captured
- Confusing document date with event date
- Relying on a timeline entry without source review

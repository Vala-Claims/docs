## Overview

Billing covers the subscription and payment information associated with your Vala organization.

## When to use

Use this page when reviewing your plan, payment details, invoices, or a billing question.

## Prerequisites

- Billing or administrative access
- The correct Vala organization

## Step-by-step

<Note>Detailed self-service billing instructions are coming soon.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the organization">Make sure the billing question applies to the active organization.</Step>
  <Step title="Gather the details">Note the invoice, date, amount, or subscription question involved.</Step>
  <Step title="Use available billing controls">Follow the options shown to your administrative role.</Step>
  <Step title="Contact support if needed">Email support@valaclaims.com without including sensitive payment credentials.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Limit billing access to authorized team members.
- Keep billing contact information current.
- Reference invoice details when asking for help.

## Common mistakes

- Reviewing billing in the wrong organization
- Sharing full payment credentials by email
- Assuming every team role can view billing

# Welcome to Vala

Vala is the AI operating system for VA disability claims. It helps accredited attorneys, claims agents, and Veteran Service Organizations review records, organize evidence, complete forms, collect signatures, and manage claim preparation in one workspace.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Analyze records" icon="file-magnifying-glass">
    Upload C-Files and supporting records, then use Vala to surface relevant evidence and answer questions across the file.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prepare claims" icon="folder-open">
    Organize conditions, generate reports, complete VA forms, and move work toward a submission-ready package.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collect signatures" icon="signature">
    Send VA forms and custom documents for electronic signature with vSign.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Work as a team" icon="users">
    Collaborate across advocates and support staff while keeping each veteran's work organized.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Start with one complete workflow

The fastest way to learn Vala is to take one veteran from record upload through a prepared claim:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the veteran">
    Add the veteran and confirm the basic profile information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload records">
    Upload the C-File and any additional medical, service, or supporting documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review with Vala">
    Ask questions, review extracted information, and identify relevant evidence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare outputs">
    Generate reports, complete forms, and send documents for signature.
  </Step>
</Steps>

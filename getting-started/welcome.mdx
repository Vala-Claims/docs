## Overview

Vala is the AI operating system for VA disability claims. It gives accredited attorneys, claims agents, Veteran Service Organizations, and their teams one place to organize claim-preparation work.

## When to use

Start here when you are new to Vala or want to understand how the main workflows fit together.

## Prerequisites

- An active Vala account
- Access granted by your organization
- Approval to work with the veteran information you use in Vala

## Best practices

- Verify information against source records before relying on it.
- Follow your organization's access, review, and retention policies.
- Use focused guides while completing the corresponding work in Vala.

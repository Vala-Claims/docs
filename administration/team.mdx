## Overview

Team administration controls who can access your Vala organization and the work available to them.

## When to use

Use Team when onboarding, reviewing, or offboarding organization members.

## Prerequisites

- The administrative access required by your organization
- Verified member identity and business need

## Step-by-step

<Note>Detailed invitation and role-management instructions are coming soon.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the request">Verify the person's identity, organization, and need for access.</Step>
  <Step title="Use the appropriate access">Follow the options available to your administrative role.</Step>
  <Step title="Verify the result">Confirm that the member has only the access required.</Step>
  <Step title="Review access regularly">Remove or change access when responsibilities change.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Apply least-privilege access.
- Review membership on a regular schedule.
- Remove access promptly during offboarding.

## Common mistakes

- Granting broader access than needed
- Inviting the wrong email address
- Leaving former team members active

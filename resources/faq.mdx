## Overview

This page collects common questions about using Vala. Product-specific answers will expand as workflows are confirmed.

## When to use

Start here when you have a general question that does not require troubleshooting a specific error.

## Prerequisites

No prerequisites are required.

## Step-by-step

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Vala?">
    Vala is an AI-powered software platform for preparing VA disability claims. It is built for accredited attorneys, claims agents, Veteran Service Organizations, and their teams.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Vala replace professional review?">
    No. Vala supports claim-preparation work. Your organization remains responsible for verifying information and applying professional judgment.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can veterans use Vala directly?">
    Vala is designed principally for professional organizations that prepare VA disability claims. Contact Vala for current product availability and eligibility.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where can I get help?">
    Browse Troubleshooting or email support@valaclaims.com.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best practices

- Search the Help Center using the product term shown in Vala.
- Include relevant context when contacting support.

## Common mistakes

- Sending sensitive veteran records in an unapproved support channel
- Relying on an answer that does not match your current role or organization setup

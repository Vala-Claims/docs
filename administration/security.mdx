## Overview

Security is a shared responsibility between Vala and every organization member.

## When to use

Use this guidance during onboarding, access reviews, security questions, or suspected account issues.

## Prerequisites

- Your organization's security and privacy policies
- Access to the correct Vala account and organization

## Step-by-step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Protect your account">Use unique credentials and do not share account access.</Step>
  <Step title="Verify organization context">Confirm where sensitive information is being added or reviewed.</Step>
  <Step title="Use approved information">Process only information your organization is authorized to use.</Step>
  <Step title="Report concerns promptly">Contact your administrator and Vala support when you suspect unauthorized access or another security issue.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Use the least access required for your role.
- Lock or sign out of unattended devices.
- Keep member access and contact information current.

## Common mistakes

- Sharing login credentials
- Uploading sensitive information to the wrong matter
- Delaying a report of suspicious activity

## Overview

Claims bring together the work needed for a particular VA disability claim or claim-preparation effort.

## When to use

Use Claims when your organization needs to organize related conditions, evidence, reports, forms, and tasks.

## Prerequisites

- The correct veteran workspace
- Your organization's direction on the claim being prepared

## Step-by-step

<Note>Detailed claim creation and status instructions are coming soon.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the veteran">Verify that the matter is attached to the correct veteran.</Step>
  <Step title="Identify the work">Follow your organization's process to define the claim-preparation scope.</Step>
  <Step title="Connect supporting work">Use the relevant evidence, conditions, reports, forms, and tasks.</Step>
  <Step title="Review the matter">Confirm completeness and accuracy before any external use.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Keep the scope clear and consistent.
- Separate verified facts from working notes or AI output.
- Use your organization's review process before submission.

## Common mistakes

- Working in the wrong veteran matter
- Treating a draft output as submission-ready
- Leaving related work disconnected or unclear

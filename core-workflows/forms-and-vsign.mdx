## Overview

Forms and vSign support document preparation and electronic signature workflows. Available forms and options may vary.

## When to use

Use this area when your matter requires a supported form, document, or signature request.

## Prerequisites

- The correct veteran and matter
- The current document required for the workflow
- Verified signer and form information

## Step-by-step

## Best practices

- Verify every populated field against a source.
- Confirm signer information before sending.
- Retain the final copy according to organization policy.

## Common mistakes

- Using the wrong form or edition
- Sending before all fields have been reviewed
- Using incorrect signer contact information

## Overview

Calls and Transcripts brings call-related information into a reviewable workflow when the feature is available to your organization.

## When to use

Use it when an authorized call or transcript is relevant to your team's work.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the feature
- Required participant notice, consent, and authorization
- The correct veteran or matter context

## Best practices

- Follow consent and privacy requirements.
- Treat automated transcription as a draft.
- Review important names, dates, and statements carefully.

## Common mistakes

- Recording or processing a call without required authorization
- Assigning a transcript to the wrong matter
- Treating transcript text as perfectly accurate

## Overview

Use this guide as a map of the core Vala workflow. The exact tools available depend on your organization and role.

## When to use

Use Quick Start during onboarding or when you want to understand the end-to-end claim-preparation flow.

## Prerequisites

- An active Vala account
- Access to the appropriate organization
- A veteran matter your organization has approved for use

## Step-by-step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm your organization">
    Make sure you are working in the correct organization before adding information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or open a veteran">
    Use the veteran workspace that corresponds to the person whose matter you are preparing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add available records">
    Add only records your organization is authorized to process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the matter">
    Use the available evidence, condition, timeline, and AI tools to support your review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare outputs">
    Create the reports, forms, or signature requests appropriate to the matter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify before use">
    Review source information and generated output before sharing, filing, or relying on it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Begin with a complete, correctly identified veteran workspace.
- Keep source review and human approval in the workflow.
- Follow your organization's naming and quality-control conventions.

## Common mistakes

<Warning>
Do not upload information to the wrong organization or veteran workspace. Confirm context before you begin.
</Warning>

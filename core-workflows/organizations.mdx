## Overview

An organization is the shared Vala environment for your team and its work.

## When to use

Use this page when confirming where work belongs or when you have access to more than one organization.

## Prerequisites

- An active Vala account
- Access to at least one organization

## Step-by-step

<Note>Detailed organization switching and setup instructions will be added after the current product workflow is confirmed.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the organization">Check the active organization before viewing or adding veteran information.</Step>
  <Step title="Review your access">Make sure the available areas match the work assigned to you.</Step>
  <Step title="Continue to the workflow">Open the appropriate veteran or administrative area.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Confirm organization context before every upload.
- Use only the access needed for your role.
- Follow your organization's data-handling policies.

## Common mistakes

- Adding information in the wrong organization
- Assuming settings and access are the same across organizations

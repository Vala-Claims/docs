## Overview

Timeline supports chronological review of information available for a veteran matter.

## When to use

Use Timeline when sequence, timing, or changes over time matter to your review.

## Prerequisites

- The correct veteran workspace
- Relevant records available for review

## Step-by-step

<Note>Detailed timeline controls and filtering instructions are coming soon.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the veteran">Make sure you are reviewing the intended matter.</Step>
  <Step title="Set the review goal">Identify the event, condition, or time period you need to understand.</Step>
  <Step title="Inspect the chronology">Review entries in sequence and open available source context.</Step>
  <Step title="Verify material events">Confirm dates and details against the underlying record.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Review surrounding events, not only isolated entries.
- Distinguish record dates from dates mentioned within a record.
- Verify consequential dates independently.

## Common mistakes

- Assuming every event is captured
- Confusing document date with event date
- Relying on a timeline entry without source review

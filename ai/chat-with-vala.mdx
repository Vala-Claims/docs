## Overview

Chat with Vala is a research aid for asking questions across a veteran's available records.

## When to use

Use it to investigate a focused question, locate relevant record context, or follow up on a reviewed finding.

## Prerequisites

- The correct veteran workspace
- The relevant records available in Vala
- A specific question or review goal

## Step-by-step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask a focused question">Include the condition, time period, event, or document type you are investigating.</Step>
  <Step title="Review the cited sources">Open the underlying pages and read the surrounding context.</Step>
  <Step title="Refine the question">Ask a follow-up when the record is incomplete, ambiguous, or inconsistent.</Step>
  <Step title="Use only verified findings">Confirm important information independently before carrying it into other work.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Ask one clear question at a time.
- Include useful context in the prompt.
- Verify material facts and conclusions against source records.

## Common mistakes

<Warning>AI can miss information or interpret a record incorrectly. Do not rely on a response without reviewing the source.</Warning>

- Asking broad questions without context
- Reading only the answer and not the citations
- Treating generated language as legal or professional advice

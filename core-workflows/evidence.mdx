## Overview

Evidence includes the source records and supporting information your team reviews during claim preparation.

## When to use

Use Evidence when adding, reviewing, or organizing source material for a veteran matter.

## Prerequisites

- The correct veteran workspace
- Authorization to process the records
- Source files approved by your organization

## Step-by-step

<Note>Detailed evidence upload, processing, and organization instructions are coming soon.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the veteran">Make sure the evidence belongs to the open veteran workspace.</Step>
  <Step title="Add approved records">Use only records your organization is authorized to process.</Step>
  <Step title="Review the available material">Confirm the expected records are present and usable.</Step>
  <Step title="Verify important findings">Open source material before relying on extracted or generated information.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Preserve clear source context.
- Review the complete record around important findings.
- Follow your organization's document naming and retention rules.

## Common mistakes

- Uploading to the wrong veteran
- Assuming an upload is complete without checking it
- Citing a summary without reviewing the source

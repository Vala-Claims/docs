## Overview

This guide covers the preparation and review points for adding your first veteran and their records.

## When to use

Use it during onboarding or the first time you add a veteran matter to Vala.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the correct Vala organization
- Authorization to process the veteran's information
- The source records approved by your organization

## Step-by-step

<Note>
Detailed screen-by-screen instructions will be added after the current product workflow is confirmed.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the organization">
    Verify that you are in the organization that owns the matter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare the veteran information">
    Gather the information your organization requires to identify the veteran correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the veteran workspace">
    Follow the in-product prompts available to your role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add approved records">
    Upload only the records associated with this veteran and matter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the result">
    Confirm that the veteran and records appear in the correct workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Check for an existing veteran before creating another workspace.
- Use your organization's approved naming and intake process.
- Review the uploaded record set for completeness.

## Common mistakes

- Adding records to the wrong veteran
- Creating a duplicate veteran workspace
- Uploading information without the required authorization

# Reports

Vala reports turn uploaded records into structured working documents for claim preparation. Available report types may vary by organization and workflow.

## Before generating a report

Confirm that the relevant records have finished processing and that the veteran profile is accurate.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the report">
    Select the report type that matches the work you are performing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the draft">
    Vala analyzes the available records and produces a structured draft.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review citations and conclusions">
    Confirm important dates, diagnoses, events, and supporting evidence against the source record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit for the matter">
    Add professional judgment, remove unsupported statements, and tailor the output before use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Reports are working drafts. They should be reviewed by an accredited representative before being relied upon or shared.
</Note>

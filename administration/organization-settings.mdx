## Overview

Organization Settings contains configuration that may affect members and workflows across your Vala organization.

## When to use

Use it when an authorized administrator needs to review or change organization-level configuration.

## Prerequisites

- The appropriate administrative role
- Approval for the intended organization-wide change

## Best practices

- Limit settings access to authorized administrators.
- Make one reviewable change at a time.
- Tell affected team members about workflow changes.

## Common mistakes

- Changing the wrong organization
- Editing a setting without understanding its scope
- Failing to verify the resulting workflow

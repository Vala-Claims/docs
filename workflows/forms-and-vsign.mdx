# Forms and vSign

Vala helps your team complete VA forms from structured veteran and claim data, then send documents for electronic signature through vSign.

## Complete a form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the form">
    Choose the current form required for the veteran's workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Populate available data">
    Use information already stored in Vala to reduce repetitive entry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review every field">
    Confirm names, identifiers, dates, selections, and narrative responses before sending or downloading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send with vSign">
    Add the required signer or signers, place signature fields, and send the request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm completion">
    Review the completed document and retain the final copy with the veteran's matter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Form requirements and editions can change. Confirm that the form and information are appropriate for the intended submission.
</Warning>

## Custom documents

Your organization may also use vSign for approved custom forms and supporting documents. Follow your organization's review and retention procedures before sending them.

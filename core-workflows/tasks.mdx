## Overview

Tasks help teams keep follow-up work visible and connected to the relevant Vala workflow.

## When to use

Use Tasks for work that needs a clear next action, owner, or follow-up point.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the relevant organization and matter
- Your team's task-management conventions

## Best practices

- Use action-oriented task titles.
- Include enough context to complete the work.
- Keep task status current.

## Common mistakes

- Creating vague tasks
- Omitting the veteran or matter context
- Marking work complete before verification

# Veterans

A veteran profile is the central workspace for the records and claim-preparation work associated with one person.

## Create a veteran

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Veterans">
    From the main navigation, open **Veterans**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the profile">
    Select **New veteran** and enter the information currently available to your organization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify extracted data">
    As records are uploaded, Vala may extract profile information. Confirm extracted values against the source documents before using them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What belongs in the veteran workspace

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Records" icon="files">
    C-Files, medical records, service records, decision letters, and supporting documents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conditions" icon="list-check">
    Existing ratings, claimed conditions, potential opportunities, and related evidence.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reports" icon="file-lines">
    Generated analyses and working documents used during claim preparation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Forms and signatures" icon="signature">
    VA forms, custom documents, signature requests, and completed copies.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
Use consistent naming and review profile details early. Clean veteran data improves form population and reduces avoidable corrections later.
</Tip>

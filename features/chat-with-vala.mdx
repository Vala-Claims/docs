# Chat with Vala

Chat with Vala helps you investigate a veteran's uploaded records without manually searching every page.

## Good questions to ask

- Where is a diagnosis first documented?
- What evidence supports a reported in-service event?
- Which records mention a specific symptom or treatment?
- What prior decisions discuss a condition?
- Are there gaps or conflicting details that need review?

## Review every answer

Vala provides citations so you can inspect the underlying record. Treat the response as a research aid, not a substitute for professional review.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask a focused question">
    Include the condition, time period, document type, or event you are investigating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the citations">
    Review the cited pages and surrounding context rather than relying only on the summary.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine the question">
    Ask follow-up questions when the record is incomplete, ambiguous, or internally inconsistent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use verified findings">
    Carry confirmed information into reports, forms, conditions, and other claim-preparation work.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
AI can miss information or interpret a record incorrectly. Verify material facts and legal conclusions before use.
</Warning>

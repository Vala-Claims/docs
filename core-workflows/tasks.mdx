## Overview

Tasks help teams keep follow-up work visible and connected to the relevant Vala workflow.

## When to use

Use Tasks for work that needs a clear next action, owner, or follow-up point.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the relevant organization and matter
- Your team's task-management conventions

## Step-by-step

<Note>Detailed task creation, assignment, and completion instructions are coming soon.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the next action">Write a specific outcome that another team member can understand.</Step>
  <Step title="Connect the context">Keep the task associated with the relevant work whenever the product allows it.</Step>
  <Step title="Follow your team process">Use the ownership and timing conventions established by your organization.</Step>
  <Step title="Close the loop">Confirm the work is complete before marking the task finished.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Use action-oriented task titles.
- Include enough context to complete the work.
- Keep task status current.

## Common mistakes

- Creating vague tasks
- Omitting the veteran or matter context
- Marking work complete before verification

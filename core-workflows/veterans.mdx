## Overview

A veteran workspace keeps the records and claim-preparation work associated with one person together.

## When to use

Use a veteran workspace whenever you review records, organize conditions and evidence, prepare outputs, or track work for that veteran.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the correct organization
- Authorization to work with the veteran's information
- The identifying information required by your organization

## Step-by-step

## Best practices

- Keep one clear workspace for each veteran.
- Verify extracted or generated information against sources.
- Follow consistent naming and review conventions.

## Common mistakes

- Creating duplicate veteran workspaces
- Adding one veteran's records to another veteran
- Relying on unverified extracted information

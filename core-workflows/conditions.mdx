## Overview

Conditions provide a focused way to organize relevant information within a veteran's claim-preparation workflow.

## When to use

Use Conditions when your team is reviewing condition-specific evidence and preparing related work.

## Prerequisites

- The correct veteran and claim context
- Reviewed source records
- Your organization's direction on the matter

  <Step title="Verify before use">Confirm material facts and professional conclusions independently.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Keep condition names and scope consistent.
- Connect findings to reviewable source material.
- Record uncertainty instead of presenting it as fact.

## Common mistakes

- Duplicating the same condition
- Mixing evidence from unrelated matters
- Treating an AI suggestion as a verified conclusion
